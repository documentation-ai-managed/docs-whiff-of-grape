## Overview

Membership is open by invitation. You can express interest via our website.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://www.whiffofgrape.ca/join`
2. Fill in the interest form with your details
3. Wait for a Member to contact you
4. Attend a guest event (if invited)
5. Formal membership requires approval

<Callout kind="info">
Not all applicants will be accepted. Attendance is by invitation only.
</Callout>
