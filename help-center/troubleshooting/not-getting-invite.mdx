---
title: Not Receiving Event Invitations
description: Fix missing email invites.
---

## Symptoms

- You are a member but do not get monthly event emails
- Past invitations arrived, but recent ones have not

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Spam/Promotions">
    ````
    Search your inbox, spam and promotions folders for emails from `events@whiffofgrape.ca`.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Contact Info">
    ````
    Log in at `https://www.whiffofgrape.ca/member` and verify your email is correct.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    ````
    Still an issue? Email support@whiffofgrape.ca for help.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
