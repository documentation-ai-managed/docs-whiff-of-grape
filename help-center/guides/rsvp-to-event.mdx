---
title: How to RSVP to an Event
description: Guide for RSVPing to a Whiff of Grape event.
---

## Overview

Members must RSVP before attending monthly events.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Invitation Email">
    ````
    Find the latest invitation from `events@whiffofgrape.ca` in your inbox.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click RSVP Link">
    ````
    Click on the RSVP button or link provided in the email.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Attendance">
    ````
    Fill out the RSVP form and click submit. You'll receive a confirmation email.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
