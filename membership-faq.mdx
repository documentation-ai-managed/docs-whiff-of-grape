## Membership Fees and Renewal Process

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How much do membership fees cost?" default-open="true">

    Membership is `{CAD $75}` annually for standard members or `{CAD $50}` for students/seniors (with proof). Fees cover all monthly events, speaker honorariums, and venue costs.

    | Tier          | Annual Fee | Eligibility                  |
    |---------------|------------|------------------------------|
    | Standard     | `{CAD $75}` | General members             |
    | Student/Senior | `{CAD $50}` | Valid ID required           |

    Pay via e-transfer to `treasurer@whiffofgrape.ca` or in-person at events.

  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I renew my membership?">

    Follow these steps to renew before your expiration date:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check Status" icon="search">
        Log in to your member portal at `https://members.whiffofgrape.ca` and view your expiry date.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Tier" icon="user-check">
        Choose standard or discounted tier if eligible.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pay Fee" icon="credit-card">
        Complete payment via the portal or e-transfer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle">
        Receive confirmation email within `{24 hours}`. Your access renews immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Event Locations and Accessibility

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Where are events held?" default-open="true">
    Monthly events occur at rotating Toronto venues like The Royal Canadian Legion (Downtown) or Gladstone Hotel (West End). Check the event calendar at `https://whiffofgrape.ca/events` for exact addresses and TTC directions.

    All locations offer wheelchair ramps and hearing loops. Contact `accessibility@whiffofgrape.ca` for specific needs.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How accessible are the events?">
    We prioritize inclusivity:

    - Elevators and ramps at all venues
    - ASL interpreters for select events (request `{2 weeks}` in advance)
    - Quiet spaces for sensory needs
    - Childcare subsidies available (`{up to $20}` per event)

    Virtual attendance options via Zoom for `{CAD $10}` extra.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

  <Tab title="In-Person" icon="map-pin">

## Cancellation and Refund Policies

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Can I get a refund for events or membership?">
    Membership: Full refund within `{14 days}` of purchase, minus `{CAD $10}` fee. No refunds after.

    Events: Cancel `{48 hours}` before for full credit toward future events. No-shows forfeit tickets.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I cancel?">
    Email `registrations@whiffofgrape.ca` with your name and confirmation number. Expect response within `{1 business day}`.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Repeated cancellations may affect future registrations. We appreciate your understanding to respect waitlists.
</Callout>

## Code of Conduct for Discussions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What rules apply during discussions?" default-open="true">
    Foster respectful dialogue:

    - Listen actively without interrupting
    - Debate ideas, not people
    - No personal attacks or harassment
    - Stay on-topic after speaker Q&A

    Violations lead to warnings, then removal. Report issues to moderators on-site.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What happens if I violate the code?">
    First offense: Private warning. Second: Event removal without refund. Repeated: Membership review.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
