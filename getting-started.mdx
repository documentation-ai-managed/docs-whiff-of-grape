## Prerequisites

Before joining Whiff of Grape, ensure you meet these simple requirements:

<Callout kind="info">

- You live in or near Toronto
- You are 19 years or older
- You have an active email address for communications

</Callout>

No prior experience needed. The club welcomes newcomers eager for intellectual discussions.

## Sign Up for Membership

Joining takes just a few minutes. Follow these steps to become a member.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Website" icon="globe">
    Navigate to the [official signup page](https://whiffofgrape.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out the Form">
    Provide your full name, email, and phone number. Select your interests from topics like philosophy, science, and current affairs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and Confirm">
    Click submit. Check your email for a confirmation link from `welcome@whiffofgrape.com`. Click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Membership Fee">
    Complete the one-time `{CAD}50` annual fee via Stripe on the secure payment page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
  Congratulations! You now receive monthly event invitations and newsletter updates.
</Callout>

## Register for Upcoming Events

Browse and register for events directly from the member dashboard.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Registration" icon="globe">
    Log in at [https://whiffofgrape.com/dashboard](https://whiffofgrape.com/dashboard). Select an event and click `Register Now`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Registration" icon="mail">
    Reply to your event invitation email with `YES, count me in for [Event Name]`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Events fill quickly, so register early. Capacity is limited to 50 members per gathering.

## What to Bring to Your First Meeting

Prepare for an engaging evening at our downtown Toronto venue.

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Notebook & Pen" icon="edit-3">
    Jot down ideas from the speaker and discussions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Name Tag" icon="user">
    Pick one up at the door. Helps spark conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Refreshments" icon="coffee">
    BYOB for wine or non-alcoholic drinks. Light snacks provided.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Meetings start at 7 PM sharp and last 3 hours.
