## Overview

Join the Toronto Whiff of Grape, a dynamic social club that brings together curious minds for monthly events featuring renowned speakers. You engage in lively discussions and debates on diverse topics, from technology and philosophy to current affairs. Established in 2015, the club fosters deep connections and idea-sharing in an inclusive environment.

## Club History

<Expandable title="Learn about our origins" default-open="true">

Whiff of Grape began in a cozy Toronto café in 2015, inspired by the need for thoughtful discourse amid fast-paced city life. Founders Sarah Lee and Marcus Patel hosted the first event on climate innovation, drawing 50 attendees. Today, you join over 500 members across events at venues like the Royal Ontario Museum.

</Expandable>
